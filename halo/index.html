Tom "Ogre 2" Ryan

Player Profile: Winningest Pro of all Time

Current Role: Coach of Evil Geniuses

Tom Ryan, more commonly known by his online alias Ogre 2, is a legend in the Halo scene. He is known simply as the GOAT.

David "Walshy" Walsh

Player Profile: 31 Time Champion

Current Role: Halo Caster

David Walsh, more commonly known by his online alias Walshy, is one of the most popular people of all time in the Halo scene.

For more information on Lethul's career visit Halo eSports Wiki

Eric "Snipedown" Wrona

Player Profile: 23 Time Champion

Current Role: Player for Team Envyus

Eric Wrona, more commonly known by his online alias Snipedown, is a very well established player in the Halo scene.

For more information on Lethul's career visit Halo eSports Wiki

Tony "Lethul" Campbell

Player Profile: 2 Time World Champion

Current Role: Player for Optic Gaming

Tony Campbell, more commonly known by his online alias Lethul, is a household name in the Halo scene today.
